Traction Power Supply Simulation & Validation Framework

A long-term, funded research project focused on the development and validation of indigenous simulation models for railway traction power supply systems.

The work was carried out as part of research activities at the Industrial Research and Consultancy Centre (IRCC), IIT Bombay.

  • Developed system-level simulation models to analyze traction power supply behavior under varying operating and load conditions.
  • Assisted in building validation frameworks to verify design assumptions, performance characteristics, and system stability.
  • Worked in a Linux-based development environment with emphasis on correctness, repeatability, and structured testing practices.
  • Collaborated with multidisciplinary teams across Electrical Engineering, Energy Science & Engineering, and Industrial Engineering & Operations Research.

Project Sponsors: High Speed Railways India Corporation (HSRCL), Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC)

Technologies & Domains: Python, system modeling, simulation, Linux, traction power systems, validation workflows
